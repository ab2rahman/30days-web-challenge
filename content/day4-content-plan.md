## What Happened

**nuruldarari** commented: "Make zoro from One Piece say something on the screen when u first visit the web" — so I made Zoro slash the entire screen.

When you open the site, you're greeted by a cinematic splash sequence:
1. Pixel art Zoro running in place with loading music and a progress bar
2. Santoryu image reveal with dramatic music
3. Zoro slashes the screen 3 times — black scar lines tear across the white page
4. The white screen shatters into fragments, revealing the dark site underneath

**The Zoro Splash Screen:**
- Canvas-based pixel sprite animator with 2 sprite sheets (running + sword attack, 6 frames each)
- Loading phase: Zoro runs in place for 5 seconds with looped background music and progress bar
- Santoryu phase: Full-screen image reveal with dramatic santoryu announcement music
- Slash phase: 3 synchronized sword slashes timed to a real slash sound effect (0.576s each)
  - Each slash leaves a persistent black scar line with blue glow across the white screen
  - Zoro grows from 0.5x to 2.0x scale, dashing across the screen with each hit
  - Sparks, screen shake, and flash effects per slash
- Shatter phase: White screen explodes into 9 fragments that fall away, revealing the dark site
- Audio: Loading music (loop), santoryu announcement, slash SFX ×3 — all with smooth fade transitions
- All audio uses real MP3 files for authentic One Piece vibes
